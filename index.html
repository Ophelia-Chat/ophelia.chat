Ophelia is not a sentient being. It is an AI-powered chatbot designed to simulate emotional intelligence and explore the potential of AI in creating meaningful human-like interactions. Interactions with Ophelia are part of an experimental project, and data may be logged anonymously to improve her capabilities.
